What we have here is a budget puzzle game with some variety of mini games and anime girls.

There is no story here so you go straight to girl/puzzle choice. You have 7 (or 11 with DLC) girls to choose from and three different mini games (one DLC only): rock-paper-scissors with but with cards, dice and actual rock-paper-scissors but where you only choose the correct option. Each puzzle has 4 stages. Each mini-game has random mechanics so no strategy involved (except the DLC mini game). Unimaginative even for a budget puzzle.

Art is what sells this title. Each girl has 5 different CG pictures and 3 simple live2D animations. Every CG has good quality and level of details but animations are only so-so. Variety of fetishes is ok but mostly vanilla so if you're not a fan of it you might want to skip it. Overall design is pretty decent but there is not much here.

Music just is. It fits the theme but is not memorable. A positive feature is voice acting. I belive most if not all girls have their own unique VA. There speak with one of three languages: Chinese, English or Japanese. This is rather uncommon among low priced games like this.

No bugs encountered.

Overall, a rather basic puzzle game with random mechanics mini-games, good art + animations and ok music and voice acting. For the asked price it is relatively decent. Can recommend: 5/10
