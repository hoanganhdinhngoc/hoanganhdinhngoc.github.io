    Description

    Congo is a minimal strategy game of area domination. Master its mechanics to overcome your rivals.

    Place your pieces to claim the ground and block the expansion of your rivals... the only rule is not to place them next to pieces of a different colour. Score the most points in each of the four rounds to be the winner!

    Play against rivals all over the Web or try yourself against your friends in offline mode.

    Online mode available for Chrome and Firefox only .

    Controls

    At your turn, click or touch in a blank dot to put a piece. You have 12 pieces each of the four rounds.
